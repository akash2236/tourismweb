Who We Are

Founded in 2010, Global Explorer began as a small team of passionate travelers with a simple mission: to help people experience the world in extraordinary ways. What started as a boutique travel agency has grown into a trusted name in adventure tourism, with offices in 12 countries and a network of local experts worldwide.

We believe travel has the power to transform lives, broaden perspectives, and create connections across cultures. Our carefully curated experiences go beyond typical tourist attractions to deliver authentic, immersive journeys that leave lasting impressions.

Every Global Explorer trip is designed with sustainability in mind, ensuring our adventures positively impact both travelers and the communities we visit.
