Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

How do I book a tour with Global Explorer?

You can book directly through our website by selecting your desired tour and following the booking process. Alternatively, you can call our booking team at +1 (800) 123-4567 or email us at bookings@globalexplorer.com for personalized assistance.

What is your cancellation policy?

We offer flexible cancellation policies that vary by tour. Most tours can be cancelled for a full refund up to 30 days before departure. Some special expeditions may have different terms, which will be clearly stated during the booking process. Please review the specific terms for your selected tour or contact our team for details.

Do you offer custom or private tours?

Yes! We specialize in creating custom itineraries tailored to your interests, schedule, and budget. Our travel designers will work with you to craft a unique experience. Contact our custom tours department at custom@globalexplorer.com to start planning your dream trip.

What safety measures do you have in place?

Your safety is our top priority. All our tours comply with local health and safety regulations. We work with trusted local partners, provide 24/7 emergency support, and ensure all guides are trained in first aid. We also monitor travel advisories and adjust itineraries as needed for safety.

How far in advance should I book my tour?

We recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance for popular destinations and peak seasons. Some exclusive experiences require booking up to a year ahead. Last-minute bookings may be possible depending on availability - contact us to check options.
